About Us

Stray Love PH is a Non-Profit Organization that was founded with the main objective of uplifting the suffering of neglected, abused, and abandoned animals in the Philippines. We aim not just to rescue and rehabilitate, but also to rehome. At present, we have more than 200 dogs in our care. It is our belief that every furry creature is God's creation deserving of a good life filled with love.

Mission

To build a community where people value animals and treat them with love, respect, and kindness. To promote the humane treatment of animals through advocacy, education, and services.

Vision

To live in a world where animals live free from suffering, abandonment, neglect, abuse, and exploitation.
